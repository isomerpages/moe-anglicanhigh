<h5>AHS UNIFORMED GROUPS TALENT ADMISSION CRITERIA</h5>
<p>We are looking for Primary Six pupils who have a deep passion and interest
in Boys’ Brigade or Girls’ Brigade to embark on a 4 years journey in Anglican
High School. Applicant must offer Chinese Language and has good overall
academic performance for P5 Year-End Examinations and P6 Assessments. He
or she should possess good leadership and communication skills.</p>
